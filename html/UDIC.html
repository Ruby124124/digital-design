Department of Creative Product Design, STUST

About the Program

Southern Taiwan University of Science and Technology (STUST) is recognized as a benchmark university by the Ministry of Education. Our Department of Innovative Product Design is committed to turning creative ideas into real-world impact.

Backed by strong educational direction, substantial funding, a complete academic system, and a mature teaching model, the program offers a rich learning environment. Our faculty—both full-time and adjunct—possess diverse design experience and specialize in all areas of product design, ensuring powerful teaching and industry-academia collaboration.

With a dedicated building for hands-on creation and research, we offer state-of-the-art facilities, fostering a vibrant, supportive academic culture. Our students have consistently demonstrated outstanding performance, positioning us as a top-tier institute for product design education in Taiwan.

We have won numerous international design awards including iF Design, Red Dot Design, Good Design Award (Japan), DNA Paris Design Awards, International Design Awards (IDA), International Busan Design Award (IBDA), and Taiwan's Golden Pin Design Award, showcasing our excellence and innovation.

Program Features and Career Prospects

Our department focuses on cultivating professionals with strong capabilities in product design, service design, and strategic design thinking. Through solid training in practical skills, collaboration, and interdisciplinary learning, students develop the ability to connect design with market and user needs. Graduates are equipped with experience in 3D modeling, prototyping, and real-world projects that align with industry requirements.

Employment Rate: 95%   |   Working in Related Fields: 80%   |   Pursuing Further Studies: 15%

Main Career Fields:

  • Product Designer – Consumer goods, functional tools, mechanical design, etc.
  • User Experience Designer – UX/UI designer, interaction designer, interface design roles in tech firms.
  • Cultural & Strategic Design – Design marketing, visual communication, branding, exhibition and promotion roles.

Further Development:

  • Startups and Innovation – Turning ideas into real businesses, becoming creative entrepreneurs.
  • Graduate Studies – Deepening knowledge in design theories and applied research for future academic or R&D roles.
  • Design Management – Leading creative teams and managing innovation in design departments or agencies.

Our strong partnerships with industry provide students with valuable internship opportunities, joint projects, and hands-on practice, ensuring students graduate with strong employability and a competitive edge.
